Our Story

A name that has found it’s own place in the life of a food-lover in Malabar, one that needs no introduction The plant that grew from a seedling planted 30 years ago, ALBAIK® is credited with introducing Authentic Arabian Food to the people of Malabar. From a restaurant in the heart of Malappuram Town to holding a market share of 60% in the Malabar region. Our systematic approach and highly professional practices of business is the secret of our growth and we have polished this quality to compete with global brands.
